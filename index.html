Hi, I'm Ave. I work on websites, email marketing, and operations support for small businesses.

How I Work

Simple, clear, no surprises.

01

Understand

Before anything else, I want to understand what you're working on and what you actually need. That usually means a short conversation where I ask questions and listen more than I talk.

02

Plan

Once the scope is clear, I'll put together a plan covering what we're doing, in what order, and by when. Nothing complicated, just enough to make sure we're on the same page before work starts.

03

Build

This is where the actual work happens. I'll keep you updated along the way and flag anything early if something comes up. You won't be left wondering where things stand.

04

Review

Before anything is final, we review it together. If something needs adjusting, that's what this step is for. Getting the details right matters more to me than moving fast.

05

Support

Once the project wraps up, I'm still here if you have questions or need something adjusted. A lot of my best work comes from ongoing relationships, not one-off projects.
