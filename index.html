How I Work

A method as considered as
the outcome.

01

Discovery

We begin with deep listening. I map your current operations, identify gaps, and understand what "success" looks like for you — not just for this project, but for your business in 12 months.

02

Planning

Before a single pixel is placed or workflow built, I deliver a detailed project plan. Scope, milestones, deliverables, and success metrics — all in writing. No surprises.

03

Execution

Work begins with regular check-ins and transparent progress updates. You're never left wondering what's happening — every milestone is visible and on schedule.

04

Optimization

Good enough is never the standard. After delivery I analyze results, gather feedback, and refine until we've exceeded the original target — then I document everything.

05

Delivery & Handoff

You receive clean, documented deliverables with full ownership. SOPs for everything. Video walkthroughs where needed. You should be able to manage it all without me — or choose to have me manage it forever.
